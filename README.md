# Where is the Math in... Molecular Symmetry Approximations
## By: Trejo Aceves Luis Enrique
Research made for understanding the purpose of the 5th power rule and the usefulness for approximating real behavior of materials
### About Me
My name is Luis Enrique Trejo Aceves, and I am a Chemistry student at Arkansas State University Campus Queretaro. My major focuses on molecular models to predict reactions, but these models are often optimized into "ideal" forms that don't perfectly match real life. This is why I'm fascinated by molecular symmetry approximations, especially in inorganic chemistry, where we work with coordination metals.
